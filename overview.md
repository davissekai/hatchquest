Project HatchQuest - Overview

Objective Statement

Project HatchQuest is the flagship experiential platform of the UGBS Nest, where aspiring student entrepreneurs hatch their true potential through an immersive, consequence-driven story. Starting with GHS 10,000 in virtual capital, players embark on a continuous entrepreneurial journey, navigating interconnected narrative beats that quietly measure their Entrepreneurial Orientation across the five core dimensions — Autonomy, Innovativeness, Proactiveness, Risk-Taking, and Competitive Aggressiveness. At the narrative’s end, HatchQuest reveals a personal radar chart and aggregate acumen score, delivering clear, actionable insight that turns raw student talent into confident, self-aware founders ready to launch from the Nest.

Problem Statement

Currently, the UGBS Nest lacks a standardized, data-driven mechanism to pre-validate students seeking entry into the hub's programs. When aspiring founders arrive, there is no objective baseline to determine their operational fit or current capability level. This creates a critical blind spot: hub administrators cannot easily identify who requires foundational training (e.g., basic workshops) versus who possesses the raw acumen ready for advanced incubation. Without this triage system, resource allocation is inefficient, and students may be mismatched with their development tracks.

Solution

HatchQuest serves as a dynamic, diagnostic gateway disguised as an engaging simulation. By immersing players in a state-driven journey that tests their business instincts against simulated real-world pressures, the platform observes and evaluates their decision-making in real-time. It uncovers hidden strengths and exposes operational blind spots without the bias of self-reporting, providing the UGBS Nest with the precise data needed to route talent effectively.

Deliverable(s)
A robust, scalable, and highly operation-efficient web-based simulation platform. The core deliverables include:
The Player Environment: An immersive, state-managed frontend where users engage with a continuous, 30-part business narrative with compounding consequences.
The Diagnostic Engine: A backend logic system that tracks the hidden impact of every plot choice on the player's virtual capital and Entrepreneurial Orientation.
The Analytics Output: A quantified end-of-game results dashboard featuring a dynamic Radar Chart, a final Entrepreneurial Acumen Score, and personalized feedback.
