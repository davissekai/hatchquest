## Design Context

### Users
University of Ghana Business School (UGBS Nest) founders and incoming entrepreneurs. They are young, ambitious, digitally fluent Ghanaians who move between academic prestige (Legon) and street-level hustle (Makola). They engage on mobile first. The job: play a serious entrepreneurship simulation that genuinely assesses their EO profile — not a toy.

### Brand Personality
**Scholarly Soul of the Streets.** Three words: Prestigious. Vibrant. Accra.
The brand rejects cold Western SaaS sterility. It blends the intellectual gravity of UG's Great Hall with the rhythmic organic chaos of Makola Market. Maximalist Precision — think a well-curated editorial spread, not a dashboard.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **System**: Material Design 3 tokens — full palette (primary #524fb2, secondary #6d5a00, tertiary #ac2c00, yellow secondary-container #fad11b)
- **Typography**: Plus Jakarta Sans (headlines, labels, buttons) + Newsreader (body, narrative, italic pull-quotes)
- **Shape**: "The Round" — xl border-radius (3rem) on all cards and buttons. No sharp corners ever.
- **Depth**: No 1px divider lines. Depth via background color shifts (surface hierarchy). Glassmorphism (backdrop-blur-xl, rgba white/dark overlays) for floating panels.
- **Color**: Light mode everywhere. Dark cinematic hero backgrounds only on Layer 0 and Play screens.
- **References**: Material You, editorial African digital publications, Legon campus photography
- **Anti-reference**: Brutalist pixel-borders, monospace retro gaming, neon dark-mode dashboards

### Design Principles
1. **No borders for sectioning.** Background color shifts define boundaries — never 1px lines.
2. **Round everything.** A sharp corner is a failure of warmth. `rounded-xl` (3rem) is the default card shape.
3. **Newsreader carries the narrative.** All story text, scenario prose, and pull-quotes use Newsreader italic. This is the "scholarly" half.
4. **secondary-container (#fad11b) is the energy color.** Use it for primary CTAs, accent highlights, and anything that should vibrate with Accra's market energy.
5. **Glassmorphism on dark backgrounds only.** `.glass-panel` (rgba white, backdrop-blur) sits on top of cinematic blurred Accra photography (Layer 0, Play screen). On light screens, use surface-container cards.
